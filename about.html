ABOUT THE TEAM

The Story Behind Cinderella 1942

OUR STORY

Team 1942 "Cinderella" is an FRC (FIRST Robotics Competition) team that brings together talented students passionate about science, technology, engineering, and mathematics.

Founded with the mission of inspiring young minds through robotics, our team has grown from a small group of enthusiasts into a full-fledged engineering organization. Every year, we design, build, and program a competitive robot in just six weeks — an incredible challenge that teaches us real-world engineering skills.

Our team name "Cinderella" represents our belief that with hard work, creativity, and teamwork, anything is possible — even turning a pumpkin into a championship-winning robot.

Our Mission

To inspire the next generation of engineers and innovators through hands-on robotics education

OUR DEPARTMENTS

Specialized teams working together to build something amazing

Mechanical

Design and build the robot's physical structure, mechanisms, and drivetrain using CAD software and hands-on fabrication.

Electronics

Wire and integrate all electrical components — motors, sensors, controllers — ensuring reliable robot operation.

Programming

Develop the robot's software including autonomous routines, driver controls, and vision processing systems.

Strategy

Analyze the game, scout competitors, and develop winning strategies for alliance selection and match play.

Media & Design

Create the team's visual identity, capture photos and videos, manage social media, and produce marketing materials.

Business & Outreach

Handle sponsorships, community engagement, STEM outreach events, and team logistics.
