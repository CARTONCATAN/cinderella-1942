DEPARTMENTS

Click on a department to learn more

Mechanical Team

Our mechanical team handles all aspects of robot design, from initial CAD concepts to final assembly. They work with CNC machines, 3D printers, and hand tools to bring designs to life.

Electronics Team

The electronics team is responsible for all wiring, sensors, and control systems. They ensure reliable communication between the robot's brain and its physical components.

Programming Team

Our programmers write the code that brings the robot to life. From autonomous routines to vision processing, they create the intelligence behind our machines.

Strategy Team

The strategy team analyzes the game rules, scouts competitors, and develops match strategies. Their work is crucial for alliance selection and on-field tactics.

Media Team

Our media team captures the team's journey through photography, videography, and graphic design. They manage social media and create promotional content.

Community Outreach

The outreach team organizes STEM events, mentors younger teams, and spreads the message of FIRST in our community through workshops and demonstrations.
