WHO WE ARE

Team 1942 Cinderella is an FRC (FIRST Robotics Competition) team combining passion, creativity, and engineering excellence. We design, build, and program robots to compete at the highest level.

Our team brings together students with diverse skills — from mechanical engineering to software development, from strategy to media — united by our love of robotics.
